About Target1st

Target1st is an independent academic preparation store for Indian competitive exam aspirants. We build affordable, exam-aligned digital study tools — interactive HTML apps, PDF guides, and practice packs — for banking, SSC, railway, police, other government exams and university exams as well.

Our Mission

To make quality exam preparation accessible to every student in India — regardless of city, background, or budget. A ₹29 tool made with care beats a ₹2000 coaching pack made for profit.

What We Offer

  • Interactive HTML practice tools — work offline in any browser
  • PDF study guides updated for current exam patterns
  • Category-wise coverage: Reasoning, Quant, English, GK, Current Affairs
  • Exam-specific packs: IBPS, SBI, SSC, Railways, Police, HSSC & more
